Aim: move your finger or mouse around the table to point the cue.
Shoot: press and HOLD on the table to charge the power meter (it sweeps 0→100→0), then let go to fire.
Spin: drag the dot on the spin pad to add follow, draw or english.
Aim guide: the white dashed line and ghost ball show where you'll strike; the colored arrow shows where the object ball will go.
Goal: pot all your group (solids or stripes), then sink the 8-ball to win. Foul and your opponent gets ball-in-hand; pot the 8 early or scratch on it and you lose.
